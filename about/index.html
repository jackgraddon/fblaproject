About Me

Jack Graddon

The Story

My name is Jackson Graddon and I am a photographer.

My passion for photography began five years ago. I have found this passion really rewarding because I see art everywhere I go. This passion has lead me to discover that I also really love graphic design!

How it Started

Five years ago, I was given my first phone with a good camera. I was amazed at the upgrade from the things I had been using to take photos prior, so I started taking pictures of various objects around my house. I was eager to learn more about how to use this camera to its full potential.

Life Now

Today I travel the world taking pictures. I decided that I want to share my fondness for photography with others, so I started my small photography business. I will take pictures of events like wedings, funerals, business parties, and more. One day I hope to be able to open an online class for aspiring photographers to learn the basics on taking, editing, and making money off of photos.
