About Me

Jack Graddon

The Story

My name is Jackson Graddon and I am a photographer.

My passion for photography began five years ago. Photography is so rewarding because I see art everywhere I go in nature, architecture, graphic design, and industrial design. I see art in families, communities, and how we interact with each other.

How it Started

Five years ago, I was given my first phone with a high quality camera. I was amazed at the upgrade from the traditional point and shoot cameras I had been using and I started taking pictures of different objects around my house. I was eager to learn more about how to use this camera to its full potential so I started playing with the maneuverability of the camera by capturing images at unique angels and from different perspectives. Then I learned more about lighting and editing, and I was hooked!

Life Now

...so I opened my own small photography studio. I love to record memories of my travels and everyday experiences through photography. Let's work together to capture moments from the most special events or milestones in your life. Contact me with your photography needs for weddings, ceremonies, parties, and family milestones. One day, I plan to offer online courses for aspiring photographers to learn the basics of taking, editing, and earning money from photography.
